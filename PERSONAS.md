# Personas

Key personas involved in the air quality case management process, from crowdsourced reporters through to DfE oversight.

---

## Crowdsourced Reporters (Case Raisers)

### 1. Parent / Carer
- **Goal:** Raise concerns about their child's learning environment and health
- **Context:** May notice their child coming home with headaches, respiratory issues, or reporting bad smells. Limited visibility of what's happening inside the building day-to-day
- **Needs:** Simple, accessible way to report concerns; transparency on what action is being taken; timely updates on resolution
- **Pain points:** Doesn't know who to contact, unclear if concerns are taken seriously, no visibility of whether other parents have the same issue

### 2. Teacher / Teaching Staff
- **Goal:** Flag air quality issues affecting their classroom and pupils' ability to learn
- **Context:** Present in the building daily, first to notice persistent issues like poor ventilation, dust, or mould. Balancing teaching responsibilities with reporting
- **Needs:** Quick reporting mechanism that doesn't take time away from teaching; confidence that facilities will act; ability to note patterns over time
- **Pain points:** Reports disappearing into a black hole, having to chase facilities teams, no feedback loop

### 3. Student (Secondary age)
- **Goal:** Report issues in spaces they use — classrooms, corridors, sports halls
- **Context:** May be more attuned to immediate symptoms (headaches, difficulty breathing during PE). Less likely to use formal channels unprompted
- **Needs:** Age-appropriate, low-friction way to flag problems; feeling that their voice matters
- **Pain points:** Not taken seriously, no awareness that reporting channels exist

---

## School-Level Management (Case Handlers)

### 4. School Business Manager / Facilities Manager
- **Goal:** Triage incoming cases, coordinate remediation, manage building maintenance
- **Context:** Responsible for the physical environment. Manages contractors, budgets, and compliance. Likely already stretched across many responsibilities
- **Needs:** Dashboard of open cases by location and severity; ability to assign and track work; evidence trail for audits; link to maintenance schedules
- **Pain points:** Ad-hoc reporting via email/phone, no single view of issues, difficulty demonstrating action to parents or governors

### 5. Headteacher / Senior Leadership
- **Goal:** Ensure pupil and staff welfare; maintain school reputation; meet duty of care obligations
- **Context:** Needs strategic oversight, not operational detail. Accountable to governors, parents, and DfE
- **Needs:** Summary view of case volumes, trends, and unresolved critical issues; evidence of action taken for governance reports
- **Pain points:** Blindsided by escalations, lack of data to justify capital expenditure requests

---

## DfE / Central Government (Oversight & Policy)

### 6. School Delivery Manager (DfE)
- **Goal:** Monitor the condition and safety of schools within their region/portfolio; identify systemic issues; support schools in resolving problems
- **Context:** Responsible for a portfolio of schools. Interfaces between schools and DfE policy/funding. Needs to spot patterns across multiple schools — e.g., a cluster of mould cases in buildings of the same era or construction type
- **Needs:**
  - Aggregated view of cases across their school portfolio
  - Ability to drill down into individual school case histories
  - Alerts when cases hit critical severity or remain unresolved beyond SLA
  - Data to support funding bids or escalation to capital programmes (e.g., School Rebuilding Programme)
  - Evidence base for prioritising interventions
- **Pain points:** Relying on schools to self-report; inconsistent data quality; no early warning system; difficulty evidencing need to policy teams

### 7. DfE Policy / Analytical Team
- **Goal:** Use aggregated, anonymised data to inform national policy on school buildings and air quality standards
- **Context:** Needs large-scale trend data, not individual cases. Interested in correlations (building age, region, construction type vs. air quality issues)
- **Needs:** Exportable datasets; national dashboards; trend analysis over time
- **Pain points:** Data locked in individual schools, no standardised reporting

## Summary Table

| Persona | Role in Process | Primary Action | Access Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| Parent / Carer | Raises cases | Submit & track own cases | Public / authenticated |
| Teacher | Raises cases | Submit, track, add evidence | School user |
| Student | Raises cases | Submit simple reports | School user (limited) |
| School Facilities Manager | Triages & resolves | Manage cases, assign work, update status | School admin |
| Headteacher / SLT | Oversight | View summaries, approve actions | School admin (read-heavy) |
| DfE Delivery Manager | Regional oversight | Monitor portfolio, escalate, evidence funding | DfE regional |
| DfE Policy / Analytics | National oversight | Aggregate analysis, trend reporting | DfE central (anonymised) |
