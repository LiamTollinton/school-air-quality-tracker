# School Air Quality Tracker

A comprehensive platform for monitoring and managing air quality in educational institutions, combining real-time data collection with crowdsourced insights and administrative oversight.

## Overview

The School Air Quality Tracker provides schools with tools to:
- Monitor air quality conditions across buildings and classrooms
- Integrate administrative data sources for environmental compliance and reporting
- Enable crowdsourced case management where community members can report air quality concerns and observations
- Track health impacts and environmental issues through collaborative case documentation

## Key Features

### Administrative Sources
- Integration with environmental monitoring systems and regulatory data
- Data aggregation from schools' administrative records and facilities management
- Support for compliance tracking and reporting requirements

### Crowdsourced Case Management
- Community-driven reporting of air quality issues and health concerns
- Collaborative case documentation and tracking
- Real-time feedback from students, staff, and families
- Case assignment and follow-up workflow for school administration
